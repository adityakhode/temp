Tarot Blachow Drone ​

Description ​

The Tarot Blachow is a versatile and powerful drone designed for professional-grade applications, including aerial photography, mapping, and industrial inspections. Known for its stability and robustness, it is well-suited for demanding environments and tasks, offering reliability and high performance.

Payload Capacity ​

  • Maximum Payload: 2.5 kg

Images ​

Flight Time ​

  • Maximum Flight Time: Approximately 30 minutes (varies with payload and battery configuration)

Application ​

The Tarot Blachow excels in professional applications such as aerial photography, surveying, inspections, and monitoring in various industries including agriculture, construction, and energy. Its rugged design ensures dependable performance in challenging conditions.

Components Used ​

  • Flight Controller: Tarot ZYX-M Flight Controller
  • Camera: Compatible with multiple camera models (e.g., GoPro, Sony RX0)
  • Motors: Tarot 4008 600KV Brushless Motors
  • Batteries: 6S 12000mAh LiPo Battery
